Who is FLEPACO for?

My consulting services support organizations facing technical or strategic decisions related to flexible packaging – regardless of company size or industry.

Typical clients include:

• Brand owners & manufacturers developing or optimizing packaging solutions
• Industrial and consumer goods companies assessing materials, costs or sustainability
• Small and medium-sized enterprises without in-house packaging specialists
• Start-ups & scale-ups industrializing or scaling packaging concepts
• Procurement, R&D and quality teams seeking independent technical assessment

Engagements are also well suited for time-critical questions or as temporary external expertise.

Services

Analysis & Consulting

Structured evaluation of packaging options considering technical requirements, risks, costs and regulatory aspects.

Materials & Specifications

Project Support
